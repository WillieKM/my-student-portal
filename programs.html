Programs of Study

From foundational Bible training to advanced theological study, Revealed Bible College offers pathways for students at different stages of their calling and ministry development.

Diploma in Christian Ministry

Foundational Program

A one-year program focused on laying a strong foundation in Scripture, doctrine, and personal discipleship.

  • Old and New Testament survey
  • Basic doctrine and Christian living
  • Introduction to ministry and service

Bachelor in Theology

Undergraduate Program

Designed to equip pastors, teachers, and church leaders with a solid theological and practical ministry framework.

  • Systematic and biblical theology
  • Hermeneutics and homiletics
  • Pastoral ministry and leadership

Graduate & Advanced Studies

Advanced Program

For students seeking deeper engagement in theology, leadership development, and ministry practice.

  • Advanced biblical and theological themes
  • Research, writing, and ministry projects
  • Specialized ministry focus areas

Ministry & Leadership Tracks

Special Focus

Targeted tracks designed for worship leaders, youth ministers, church planters, and ministry practitioners.

Program availability and delivery formats vary. Contact admissions for current offerings.
