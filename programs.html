Programs

Study pathways at RBC

From foundational Bible training to advanced theological study, RBC offers pathways for students at different stages of their calling and ministry development.

Diploma in Christian Ministry

Foundational Program

A one-year program focused on laying a strong foundation in Bible, doctrine, and personal discipleship. Ideal for new leaders, ministry volunteers, and believers who want to grow deeper in the Word.

  • Old and New Testament survey
  • Basic doctrine and Christian living
  • Introduction to ministry and service

Bachelor in Theology

Undergraduate Program

A deeper track designed to equip pastors, teachers, evangelists, and church leaders with a solid theological and practical ministry framework.

  • Systematic and biblical theology
  • Hermeneutics and homiletics
  • Pastoral ministry and leadership

Graduate & Advanced Studies

Advanced Program

For those who have completed previous training and desire to explore theology, leadership, and ministry practice at a more advanced level.

  • Advanced biblical and theological themes
  • Research, writing, and ministry projects
  • Specialized ministry focus areas

Ministry & Leadership Tracks

Special Focus

Selected programs and tracks tailored for worship leaders, youth workers, church planters, and other ministry roles.

For details on current offerings, availability, and formats (online, part-time, or blended), please contact the admissions office.
