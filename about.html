About Revealed Bible College

Revealed Bible College is committed to training believers in biblical truth, spiritual maturity, and global leadership. Our programs are designed to equip students with a strong theological foundation while fostering spiritual growth and ministry readiness.

Our Mission

To prepare men and women for Spirit-led ministry by providing Christ-centered education rooted in Scripture, prayer, and practical application.

Our Vision

To see transformed leaders serving effectively in churches, organizations, and mission fields around the world.

Core Values

  • Authority of Scripture
  • Spiritual Formation
  • Academic Excellence
  • Global Engagement
