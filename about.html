About Revealed Bible College

A legacy of biblical fidelity and service

Revealed Bible College is a Pentecostal, charismatic training center dedicated to helping believers know the Word of God, hear His voice, and serve with humility and excellence in every area of life.

Our History and Vision

Revealed Bible College began with a simple desire: to see believers grounded in Scripture and equipped to minister in the power of the Holy Spirit. Over time, that small training effort has grown into a global community of students, graduates, and ministry partners.

Our vision is to see every graduate equipped not just with knowledge, but with the character and practical skills needed to lead, preach, teach, and serve God’s people in a complex world.

Core Values

Scriptural Authority

We believe the Bible is the final authority in all matters of faith and life.

Servant Leadership

Graduates are trained to lead from a posture of humility, integrity, and service.

Community & Discipleship

Growth happens in community through mentoring, accountability, and authentic relationships.

Academic Rigor

We are committed to excellence in scholarship, critical thinking, and clear communication.

Accreditation & Reach

Revealed Bible College partners with churches, ministries, and networks in multiple regions to deliver accessible, Spirit-filled theological training. Accreditation and formal recognition may vary by region and partner.
