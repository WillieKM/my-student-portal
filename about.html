About Revealed Bible College

Who we are and why we exist

Revealed Bible College is a Pentecostal, charismatic training center dedicated to helping believers know the Word of God, hear His voice, and serve with humility and excellence in every area of life.

Our Mission

Our mission is to raise a generation of men and women who are firmly grounded in Scripture, sensitive to the Holy Spirit, and equipped with practical ministry skills. We aim to see our students transformed in character, mindset, and lifestyle so they can influence their churches, communities, and nations.

Our Vision

We envision a vibrant community of graduates whose lives clearly reflect Christ—leaders who are marked by integrity, compassion, spiritual maturity, and a deep love for people and the local church.

Spirit-led formation

Teaching, mentorship, and community life are designed to nurture spiritual growth and Christlike character.

Biblical foundations

Courses anchor students in sound doctrine and a high view of Scripture as the final authority.

Practical ministry

We emphasize real-world application: teaching, preaching, pastoral care, missions, and marketplace witness.

Revealed Bible College

A community of learning, worship, and transformation for those called to serve God and His people.
