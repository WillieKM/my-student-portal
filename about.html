Admissions

Admissions is more than an application process — it is a step of faith toward preparation for ministry, leadership, and service.

Who Should Apply

Students sensing a calling to grow in biblical knowledge, ministry leadership, or Christian service. Applicants may include pastors, ministry leaders, church workers, missionaries, and believers seeking deeper biblical understanding.

Admissions Process

  1. Review academic programs
  2. Submit an inquiry to begin your application journey
  3. Application review by our admissions team
  4. Enrollment and next steps

General Requirements

Decisions consider spiritual maturity, Christian character, and readiness for academic study. Applicants should demonstrate willingness to grow spiritually, academically, and personally.

Contact Admissions

If you believe God is leading you to take the next step, we encourage you to begin a conversation with our admissions team today.
