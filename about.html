About Revealed Bible College

A Christ-centered institution dedicated to biblical truth, spiritual formation, and ministry leadership.

Revealed Bible College exists to equip men and women with a deep understanding of Scripture and a strong foundation for Spirit-led ministry. Our commitment is to academic integrity, faithful teaching, and practical application of God’s Word.

We believe that biblical education should form both the mind and the heart. Our programs are designed to cultivate theological clarity, personal maturity, and a passion for serving the Church and the nations.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that shape our academic and spiritual life.

Our Mission is to prepare Christ-centered leaders through rigorous biblical education, spiritual formation, and practical ministry training.

Our Vision is to see graduates serve faithfully in churches, ministries, and communities around the world, advancing the Gospel with wisdom, humility, and conviction.

Our Core Values

The commitments that define who we are and how we teach.

Biblical Authority

Scripture is the foundation of all teaching, learning, and ministry practice at Revealed Bible College.

Spiritual Formation

We emphasize personal growth, character development, and a life shaped by prayer and obedience.

Servant Leadership

Our programs encourage humility, integrity, and a commitment to serving others faithfully.

Leadership & Faculty

Experienced practitioners committed to teaching and discipleship.

Revealed Bible College is led by pastors, teachers, and ministry leaders with years of experience in theological education and church service. Our faculty brings together academic preparation and real-world ministry insight to guide students effectively.

Through mentorship, instruction, and example, our leadership team seeks to model faithful service, doctrinal soundness, and a heart for the global Church.
