About Revealed Bible College

Training believers in biblical truth, spiritual formation, and leadership for service across cultures and nations.

Our Mission

Revealed Bible College exists to equip men and women with a deep understanding of Scripture, a strong foundation in Christian doctrine, and the practical skills required for effective ministry and leadership. We are committed to preparing students to serve faithfully in churches, missions, education, and community leadership roles worldwide.

Our Vision

Our vision is to raise a generation of Spirit-led leaders who are grounded in biblical truth, guided by integrity, and empowered to impact the world through the Gospel of Jesus Christ. We seek to foster academic excellence, spiritual maturity, and global awareness in every student we serve.

Core Values

Biblical Authority

We uphold the Bible as the inspired and authoritative Word of God, forming the foundation for all teaching, learning, and ministry.

Spiritual Formation

We emphasize spiritual growth through prayer, worship, discipleship, and the development of Christ-like character.

Academic Excellence

We pursue rigorous theological education delivered with clarity, relevance, and academic integrity.

Global Impact

We prepare students to engage diverse cultures and contexts with humility, compassion, and a heart for mission.

Begin Your Journey

Whether you are called to pastoral ministry, missions, leadership, or deeper biblical study, Revealed Bible College offers pathways to support your calling.
