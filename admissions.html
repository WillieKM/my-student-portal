Who Should Apply

Revealed Bible College welcomes students who sense a calling to grow in biblical knowledge, ministry leadership, and Christian service. Applicants may include pastors, ministry leaders, church workers, missionaries, and believers seeking deeper biblical understanding.

Admissions Process

  1. Review academic programs
  2. Submit an inquiry to begin your application journey
  3. Application review with our admissions team
  4. Enrollment and next steps

General Requirements

Admissions decisions consider spiritual maturity, commitment to Christian values, and readiness for academic study. Applicants should demonstrate a willingness to grow spiritually, academically, and in Christian character.

Preparing You for Ministry and Leadership

Our admissions process is designed to support spiritual discernment, academic readiness, and personal growth. We are honored to walk alongside students as they prepare to serve the Church and impact the world through Christ-centered leadership.
