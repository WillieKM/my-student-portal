Who Should Apply

We welcome individuals called to ministry, Christian leadership, biblical studies, missions, and service. This includes pastors, missionaries, church workers, educators, youth leaders, marketplace ministers, and believers seeking spiritual growth.

Admissions Process

  1. Explore our programs to find the right fit.
  2. Submit an inquiry to begin your application.
  3. Admissions review by our leadership team.
  4. Acceptance & enrollment with guidance for next steps.

Requirements

All applicants are evaluated based on spiritual maturity, Christian character, readiness for learning, and a heart to grow in knowledge and service. No prior ministry experience or degree is required.
