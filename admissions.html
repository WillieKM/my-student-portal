Admissions

Admissions is not just an application process—it is a step of faith toward preparation for ministry, leadership, and Christian service.

Who Should Apply

We welcome individuals who sense a calling to grow in biblical knowledge, Christian leadership, and ministry. This includes pastors, missionaries, church workers, ministry leaders, and believers seeking deeper biblical understanding.

Admissions Process

  1. Explore our programs to find the right path for your calling
  2. Submit an inquiry to begin the application process
  3. Application review by the admissions team
  4. Acceptance, enrollment, and orientation

General Requirements

All applicants are evaluated based on spiritual maturity, Christian character, readiness for academic study, and a willingness to grow spiritually, academically, and personally.

Ready to Take the Next Step?

If you believe God is leading you to pursue biblical studies, ministry training, or Christian leadership education, our admissions team would be honored to walk with you.
