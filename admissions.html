Admissions

Admissions is more than an application — it’s a step of faith toward preparation for ministry, leadership, and global service.

Who Should Apply

Revealed Bible College welcomes students called to grow in Bible knowledge, ministry leadership, and Christian service. Pastors, ministers, ministry leaders, church workers, and believers seeking biblical understanding are encouraged to apply.

General Requirements

Admissions considers spiritual maturity, commitment to Christian values, and readiness for academic study. We seek students willing to grow spiritually, academically, and in Christian character.
