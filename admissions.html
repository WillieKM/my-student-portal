Admissions

At Revealed Bible College, admissions is more than an application. It is a step of faith toward preparation for ministry, leadership, and service.

Who Should Apply

Revealed Bible College welcomes students sensing a calling to grow in biblical knowledge, ministry leadership, and Christian service. Pastors, ministers, church workers, and believers seeking deeper biblical understanding are encouraged to apply.

Admissions Process

  1. Review academic programs
  2. Submit an inquiry
  3. Application review
  4. Enrollment & next steps

General Requirements

Applicants should demonstrate spiritual maturity, commitment to Christian values, academic readiness, and a willingness to grow spiritually and academically.

Begin Your Journey

If you believe God is leading you to take the next step in ministry preparation, we encourage you to begin the conversation today.
