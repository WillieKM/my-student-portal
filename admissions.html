Admissions

At Revealed Bible College, admissions is more than an application process. It is a step of faith toward preparation for ministry, leadership, and service.

Who Should Apply

Revealed Bible College welcomes students who sense a calling to grow in biblical knowledge, ministry leadership, and Christian service.

  • Individuals called to ministry or leadership
  • Pastors, ministers, and church workers
  • Christian leaders seeking theological training
  • Believers desiring deeper biblical understanding

Admissions Process

Step 1: Review Programs

Explore our academic programs and identify the track that aligns with your calling.

Step 2: Submit Inquiry

Contact our admissions office to request information and guidance.

Step 3: Application Review

Your application is prayerfully reviewed for readiness and alignment.

Step 4: Enrollment

Once accepted, you will receive enrollment instructions and next steps.

General Requirements

Admissions decisions are based on spiritual maturity, commitment to Christian values, and readiness for academic study.

  • Personal testimony of faith
  • Commitment to Christian conduct
  • Ability to participate in academic coursework
  • Willingness to grow spiritually and academically

Begin Your Journey

If you believe God is leading you to take the next step in ministry preparation, we encourage you to begin the admissions conversation today.
